CASSO

Social Media Art Marketplace

CASSO is a project created by Alvin Dang for his CMPE 133 at San Jose State University.

Realizing the inefficient process for people to find and commission artists, Alvin envisioned a social media platform that would streamline the entire commission process from start to finish. Enabling artists around the world to abandon the limits of physical constraints as they turn a new leaf and partake in a vibrant atmosphere of creativity.

Alvin Dang

A junior at San Jose State University, Alvin is a Software Engineering major with a passion for frontend. In his free time, Alvin enjoys listening to music, snowboarding, and cooking.
